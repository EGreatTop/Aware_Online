About us

Aware Online School, a product of Aware Educational Services started as a One-on-One learning platform due to the emergence of the COVID-19 pandemic in March 2020. We effectively commenced online classes with a student in Senior Secondary School two (Year 11) who needed to continue learning during the National lock down as a result of the pandemic.

We put in our best ever since and this effort has yielded remarkable results up till date. As a result of our excellent delivery during our one-on-one classes, we have been able to earn the trust and good will of friends, associates and well meaning Nigerians both home and abroad to tutor their children via our online school programme. This is fast making Aware Online School an International brand as we have already cut across so many states in Nigeria.

Our aim is to ensure that students around the globe keep learning from the comfort of their homes which is why we can boldly say that Aware online school is one of the most cost-effective online school in Nigeria which is confirmed by the testimony of our parents and students.

The visionary: Mr Ayoola Ogunware, an erudite scholar who is a Mathematics and Science specialist has taught in different top notch Schools in Lagos from Primary to Secondary school level for more than a decade. He holds a degree in Microbiology from the prestigious Obafemi Awolowo University, Ile-Ife. He has attended many conferences and seminars within and outside Nigeria. His quest for knowledge spurred him to enrol for online courses in 2020 and that has earned him a Certified Microsoft Innovative Educator (MIE).

We serve as an intermediary between qualified teachers and parents in solving educational problems and delivering exceptional content using technology. Check out what we offer or register your wards to join a class soonest. We will be glad to have your children join us.
