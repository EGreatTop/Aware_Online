About Aware Educational Services

Aware Online school, a product of Aware Educational Services started as a One-on-One learning platform with the emergence of COVID-19 pandemic in March 2020. We commenced with one student in Senior Secondary School two (Year 11) who needed to continue learning during the National lock down as a result of the pandemic.

The results today are phenomenal as we have more students across the country.
